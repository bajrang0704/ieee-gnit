About Gurunanak Institute Of Technology

Located in India, GNIT offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and management. The institute places a strong emphasis on academic excellence, research, and innovation, preparing students to meet the evolving demands of the industry.

About IEEE GNIT

The IEEE GNITC Chapter fosters a vibrant research culture on campus by providing a platform for students to develop their technical prowess. Through workshops, hackathons, seminars, and competitions, the chapter equips students with valuable skills and fosters a space for them to showcase their research and projects.
